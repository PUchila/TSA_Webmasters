How the hub works

The Texas Directory Hub is designed as a real community tool. It brings together trusted, up-to-date resources and makes them easy to explore.

1. Discover resources

Use the directory to search by keyword, filter by category, and explore the interactive map to see what is available near you.

2. Access support

Each resource card includes a short description, location, and direct link so people can quickly contact the organizations they need.

3. Strengthen the hub

Community members and organizations can submit new resources, keeping the directory current and expanding the impact across Texas.

About the Texas Directory Hub

The Texas Directory Hub was created as a Community Resource Directory Hub for a national student technology competition. While it is a student-built project, it is designed and structured as if it were a real public service platform that communities could rely on.

Our mission is to bring together verified information about food assistance, healthcare, housing, mental health, education, and community programs into one place. By combining search tools, category filters, and a statewide map, we make it easier for people to discover resources they might otherwise miss.

How communities benefit

  • Individuals and families can quickly find local support when they face a challenge.
  • Schools and counselors can use the hub to share trusted options with students and families.
  • Nonprofits and agencies can reach more people by having their resources shown alongside partners across Texas.
